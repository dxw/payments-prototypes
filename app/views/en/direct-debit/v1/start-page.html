Pay your tax bill by Direct Debit

Set up a Direct Debit payment with Welsh Revenue Authority (WRA).

Taxes you can pay with Direct Debit:

  • Land Transaction Tax

To use this service you'll need to know:

  • Unique Transaction Reference Number
  • Postcode of the property tax is due on

You must have already filed a tax return for a purchase of property in Wales, before you can pay your bill.
