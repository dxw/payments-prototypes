Renew API connection

The team realised while designing how staff my view and match bank transaction, that they would likely need to go between it and different applications, such as check balance.

In the near future, there could feasibly be other new financial applications, such as those for tracking the dunning process [debt communications] or financial reporting.

With that in mind, the team wondered whether there was a global header to navigate between different applications.

Thinking back to what the service called this global header, the team remembered a name suggested early on for check balance - ‘Talu’ which is Welsh for ‘pay’.
